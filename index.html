City of Dreams

Top 5 Unforgettable Things to Do in Mumbai

By Jameel Jamali • Published on April 2, 2026

From the salty breeze of the Arabian Sea to the poetic chaos of its ancient bazaars, Mumbai is a city that never pauses. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a returning wanderer, here is your curated guide to the soul of Bombay.

01

Walk the Queen's Necklace at Twilight

Begin your journey at Marine Drive. As the sun dips below the horizon, the sweeping C-shaped boulevard lights up, resembling a string of glowing pearls against the dark ocean. It is the perfect place to sit, sip cutting chai, and watch the city breathe.

02

Marvel at the Gateway of India

Standing tall at the edge of the water, this basalt arch is Mumbai's most iconic monument. Built to welcome royalty, it now welcomes thousands of dreamers daily. Right across from it sits the magnificent Taj Mahal Palace Hotel—a masterpiece of architecture.

03

Hunt for Antiques in Colaba Causeway

A sensory overload in the best way possible. This bustling street market is a treasure trove of oxidized jewelry, vintage brass items, bohemian clothing, and rare artifacts. Don't forget to haggle, and wrap up your shopping with a cold beer at the legendary Cafe Mondegar.

04

Cross the Bandra-Worli Sea Link

An engineering marvel that connects the suburbs to South Mumbai. Driving across these steel cables suspended over the open sea offers a futuristic, breathtaking view of the city's ever-growing skyline. Best experienced with the windows down and the music up.

05

Explore the Art District of Kala Ghoda

The cultural heartbeat of the city. Stroll through streets lined with heritage buildings, contemporary art galleries, boutique cafes, and the famous Jehangir Art Gallery. If you visit in February, the entire precinct transforms into a massive open-air arts festival.
